```
Dear Mr. Barbic,

I'm a reporter working on a story about lobbying of the U.S. Department of
Agriculture by former USDA officials. Our review of public Lobbying
Disclosure Act filings and USAspending.gov records indicates that:

- You served in a senior role at USDA until approximately January 2021;
- You are now a registered lobbyist at Invariant LLC, where Senate LDA
  filings show 108 of your 137 filings (about 79%) target USDA; and
- Among your USDA-targeting clients, Global Clean Energy Holdings received a
  $32,260,500 USDA Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS)
  Climate-Smart Commodities cooperative agreement during 2021–2026, per
  USAspending.gov. Unlike most of the federal dollars flowing to USDA
  lobbying clients in this period — which run through routine AMS, FAS, or
  FSA programs — NRCS Climate-Smart Commodities is a discretionary,
  competitively-awarded program.

Before publishing, I want to give you the chance to respond. Specifically:

1. Is our description of your former USDA role and current lobbying accurate?
   Specifically, your departure date from USDA — we currently have
   "approximately January 2021"; please pin to the month if you can.
2. We have a residual timing question: between your departure from USDA and
   your registration as a lobbyist representing USDA-facing clients, did you
   have any contact with USDA on behalf of any private party during calendar
   year 2021? This is a §207 cooling-off question.
3. Does Invariant LLC have any process to screen your USDA work against the
   permanent restriction in 18 USC §207(a)(1) on matters you personally and
   substantially handled in government? Specifically with respect to the
   Climate-Smart Commodities program or any earlier conservation-program
   policy work: did you personally and substantially work on the program,
   regulatory framework, or any specific grant award now benefiting Global
   Clean Energy or another client?
4. How would you characterize the relationship between your prior USDA
   service and Invariant's current USDA practice?
5. Anything else you'd like on the record?

I'd be grateful for a reply by [DEADLINE — 5 business days from send]. I'm
happy to take a written statement or talk by phone.

Thank you,
[Reporter signature]
```
